ABOUT SPACE CATS

PURPOSE


“Save the Space Cats!” is a website designed for elementary students ages eight to twelve to give them basic computing knowledge. Specifically it teaches kids four lessons that include- computer parts and their names, types of computer parts, programming languages and how to write a line of code. Computer Science is a growing field and we need to give the next generation the best tools to succeed and grow.
